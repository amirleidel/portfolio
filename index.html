About me

I’m Amir, a physicist by training and a developer by passion. I enjoy learning new things, brainstorming new projects and interacting with like-minded people.

In my projects I like to explore the intersection of physics, programming, and system design. I enjoy reading into things and building them from first principles. Whether that’s a custom 2D isometric game engine in JavaScript, embedded hardware systems with microcontrollers, or tools that translate mathematical structure into interactive applications.

What I'm doing
